Cookies and Web Beacons

Sefarz uses cookies and web beacons to enhance user experience and collect information to provide personalized services to our users. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. These cookies allow us to recognize your browser and remember your preferences and settings for your next visit. We also use cookies to track your activity on our website, such as the pages you visited and the links you clicked, to improve our website's performance and functionality.

Web beacons are small electronic files that enable us to track your behavior on our website, such as which pages you visited and the time you spent on them. This information helps us to personalize our services and improve your experience on our website.

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies and web beacons as described in our Privacy Policy. You may choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but please note that some features of our website may not function properly without cookies.
